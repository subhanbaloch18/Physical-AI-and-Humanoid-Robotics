# Chapter 6: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Course Overview

- **Focus & Theme**: The intersection of AI and physical robotics, with a theme of building and training AI agents that can interact with the physical world.
- **Goal**: Students will gain comprehensive understanding of physical AI and humanoid robotics principles and practices.
- **Quarter Overview**: Four main modules culminating in a capstone project.

## Modules

### Module 1: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)

- Introduction to ROS 2
- ROS 2 Architecture
- Creating ROS 2 Packages
- ROS 2 Nodes, Topics, Services, and Actions

### Module 2: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)

- Introduction to Simulation
- Building a Digital Twin in Gazebo
- Integrating Gazebo with ROS 2
- Advanced Simulation with Unity

### Module 3: The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac)

- Introduction to NVIDIA Isaac
- Isaac Sim and Isaac Gym
- Training a Robot in Isaac Sim
- Reinforcement Learning with Isaac Gym

### Module 4: Vision-Language-Action (VLA)

- Introduction to VLAs
- Building a VLA Model
- Training a VLA Model
- Deploying a VLA Model on a Robot

### Capstone Project

Team-based project to design, build, and train a physical AI agent for complex tasks.

## Why Physical AI Matters

Physical AI is the next frontier in artificial intelligence. It represents the key to unlocking the full potential of AI and robotics. By combining advanced AI techniques with physical embodiment, we can create machines that truly understand and interact with the real world.

## Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

- Design and build a physical AI agent
- Train an AI agent to perform complex tasks
- Deploy an AI agent on a physical robot
- Understand the core principles of physical AI and humanoid robotics

## Hardware Requirements

### Digital Twin Workstation (~$3,000)

| Component | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| GPU | NVIDIA RTX 3080 or higher |
| CPU | Intel Core i9 or AMD Ryzen 9 |
| RAM | 32GB or higher |
| OS | Ubuntu 20.04 or higher |

### Physical AI Edge Kit (~$2,000)

| Component | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| SoC | NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin |
| Sensors | Camera, LiDAR, IMU |
| Voice Interface | Microphone, Speaker |

### Robot Lab Options

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Proxy | Low-cost robot for basic tasks |
| Miniature Humanoid | Small-scale humanoid for advanced tasks |
| Premium | Full-sized humanoid for research and development |

### Cloud-native Ether Lab Alternative

Available for students without access to the required hardware.
